    When not to use transformers

    Important lecture caveat.

    • If the sequence is short and the dataset is limited, a compact recurrent model can be easier to train and deploy.
    • If forecasts must run on highly constrained edge hardware, linear or compact recurrent baselines may be better.
    • If the signal is dominated by process knowledge and there is little data, hybrid or physically informed models may matter more than architecture scale.
    • If interpretability must be strongly causal and operationally auditable, simpler baselines can sometimes be the safer first choice.
