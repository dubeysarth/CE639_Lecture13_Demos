Bahdanau Attention for Streamflow Forecasting

A 30-day input window of rainfall, temperature, and soil moisture is used to produce a 7-day streamflow forecast. The central teaching question is simple: what happens when the decoder must rely on one fixed context vector c, and how does Bahdanau attention replace that bottleneck with a horizon-specific context ct?

Synthetic catchment story

This toy basin experiences two rainfall events: an earlier storm that wets the catchment and builds storage, followed by a later, stronger storm that drives a delayed runoff pulse. Streamflow responds with lag, then recedes, but the tail remains elevated because soil moisture and baseflow memory keep feeding the channel.
