Positional Encoding & Hydrological Cycles

Transformers have no built-in notion of time order. Sinusoidal positional encodings inject temporal structure by assigning each position a unique signature across d dimensions. Low-frequency dimensions capture seasonal cycles; high-frequency ones mark individual days.

Hydrological Relevance
Dim 0 oscillates every ~6 positions — encoding diurnal PET cycles. Dim 2 has a period of ~40 days — capturing monsoon onset. Dim 4 (period ~250 days) separates wet from dry seasons. The model never sees a calendar — it infers seasonality purely from these waveforms.
