Causal Masking & Autoregressive Decoding

The Transformer decoder generates one day's streamflow at a time. Causal masking ensures each prediction uses only past data — exactly like physical causality in rainfall-runoff processes. Step through the decoding process or play the animation.

Why Causal Masking?
Without masking, the decoder at training position t+3 could attend to t+5 — learning a trivial shortcut rather than the underlying rainfall-runoff dynamics. The causal mask blocks all future positions (upper triangle = −∞), forcing the model to predict each day from past information only.
